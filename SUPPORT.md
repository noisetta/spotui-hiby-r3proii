# Support

This project is maintained by one person as a community research/modding project.

Issues, questions, and pull requests are welcome, but responses are not guaranteed. I may not always have the time, hardware, or capacity to troubleshoot individual devices.

Optional donations may support test hardware, documentation, maintenance, and continued experimentation. Donations do not purchase firmware, Spotify access, device support, warranty, priority fixes, private builds, or guaranteed features.

Nothing in this project is paywalled.
